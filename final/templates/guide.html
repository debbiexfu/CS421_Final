Simple Guide

5 Tips and Tricks to Shopping on 玫瑰小舖

Refrain From Buying Items In The Sizes You Want To Fit In

Our store allows for all individuals in all sizes to select and choose items that best fit them. Simply choose items that are in your size. To avoid the hassle, buy clothings that you are most comfortable in.

Look At ALL Colors Available

We design clothing in materials that are available to us. Navigate through all colors to see which color of clothing you like best.

Use Our Measurements Chart

When applicable, use our measurement charts. Our charts can be used for all individuals in sizes S-XL

Expect your Expectations

If you are a Medium Sized indivudal, select clothes in that corresponding size. Set your expectations to your comfort and not to what society wants you to be viewed as.

Be Patient

Our orders may take a while to process due to the high demand. Please be patient and understanding of our hard workers. Though some products may take a shorter amount of time than others, please refrain from any harmful actions that may cause the site to shut down.
